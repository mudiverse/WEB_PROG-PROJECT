About the Department

The Department of Science & Technology at Orange Valley University is dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in fields such as computer science, engineering, biotechnology, and environmental science. Our state-of-the-art labs and research facilities provide students with hands-on experience and opportunities to work on groundbreaking projects.

Our faculty members are renowned experts in their fields, and our graduates are highly sought after by top companies and research institutions worldwide.
