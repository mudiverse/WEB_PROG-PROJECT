About the Program

The Computer Science program at Orange Valley University prepares students for careers in software development, artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity. Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in computer science principles while allowing students to specialize in areas of interest.

Students have access to cutting-edge labs, industry mentors, and internship opportunities with leading tech companies.
